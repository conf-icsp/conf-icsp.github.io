Workshop

There will be a workshop on November 20, 2024 for 3 hours including lectures and tutorials. Workshop details are given below .

Title of the workshop : Introduction to Quantum Cryptanalysis with Shor Algorithm

Objectives : Quantum cryptanalysis is an area of quantum computing that focuses on using quantum algorithms to break classical and post-quantum cryptographic schemes. The need for quantum cryptanalysis arises from the fact that quantum computers have the potential to solve certain mathematical problems much faster than classical computers, which could pose a threat to the security of existing cryptographic systems. Therefore, the need for quantum cryptanalysis arises from the significant computational power that quantum computers may bring, potentially rendering current cryptographic systems vulnerable. The objective of the workshop "Introduction to Quantum Cryptanalysis with Shor Algorithm" is to provide participants with a comprehensive understanding of quantum computing principles and their application in breaking classical cryptographic schemes, particularly through Shor's algorithm. The audience will learn how Shor's algorithm exploits quantum Fourier transforms to efficiently factor large numbers, thus undermining the security of widely used cryptographic protocols like RSA.


Contents :
  • Basics of Quantum Computing
  • Quantum Fourier Transform
  • Integer Factorization Problem
  • Shor Algorithm
  • Introduction to Qiskit
  • Implementation of Shor Algorithm on IBM Qiskit Platform
