About ICSP 2025

4th International Conference on Security & Privacy (ICSP 2025) will take place at Department of Mathematics National Institute of Technology Rourkela Rourkela 769008, India, during December 05-07, 2025. The ICSP 2025 aims to bring together researchers and experts in the field of security and privacy to share ideas, techniques, tools and experiences.
