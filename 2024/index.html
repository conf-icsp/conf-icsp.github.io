About ICSP 2024

3rd International Conference on Security & Privacy (ICSP 2024) will take place at National Institute of Technology Jamshedpur, India, during November 20-21, 2024 in hybrid mode. The ICSP 2024 aims to bring together researchers and experts in the field of security and privacy to share ideas, techniques, tools and experiences.
