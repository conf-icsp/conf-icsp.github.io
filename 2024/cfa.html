Call for Abstract

Extended abstracts as PDF (within 3 pages, including bibliography) on all aspects of Security and Privacy are solicited for submission to ICSP 2024 for presentation only. All Abstracts to be submitted to the 3rd International Conference on Security and Privacy will be peer reviewed by the reviewers. The single blind reviewing process will be adopted where the reviewers names will not be disclosed to the authors before or after the abstract review process. However, the authors names will be known to the reviewers as their names appear on the submitted abstracts.

Please note that at least one author of each accepted abstract must register to the conference and give a presentation at the conference in hybrid mode. Also note that acceptance of abstracts means presentation at the conference only, not publication in the conference proceedings.
