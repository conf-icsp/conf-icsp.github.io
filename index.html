The International Conference on Security & Privacy (ICSP) aims to bring together researchers and experts in the field of security and privacy to share ideas, techniques, tools, and experiences. The conference focuses on the security and privacy of publicly shared information and provides a strong platform for collaboration, professional development, and future research directions.

ICSP Core Committee

The ICSP Core Committee is responsible for the overall planning, coordination, and successful execution of the conference.
